# ✍️ Schreiben

## 💬 Freunde

Schreiben Sie:

- Was ist für Freunde wichtig?
- Was machen Freunde zusammen?
- Warum sind Freunde wichtig?

---

<details style="background:#fff4f8;border:1px solid #efcad8;border-radius:16px;padding:16px;margin-top:18px;">
<summary><b>💡 Hint for Writing</b></summary>

Write simple sentences about friendship.

🎯 You should write:
1️⃣ important qualities  
2️⃣ activities with friends  
3️⃣ reason why friends are important  

---

### Helpful vocabulary

- <b>ehrlich</b> = honest  
- <b>loyal</b> = loyal  
- <b>zusammen lernen</b> = study together  
- <b>zusammen kochen</b> = cook together  
- <b>helfen</b> = help  

---

### Strategy

Write:
✅ one sentence about qualities  
✅ one sentence about activities  
✅ one sentence about importance  

</details>
