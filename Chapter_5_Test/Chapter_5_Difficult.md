# ✍️ Schreiben

## 👨‍👩‍👧 Familie beschreiben

Schreiben Sie:

- Wer ist in Ihrer Familie?
- Wie sind die Personen?
- Was machen Sie zusammen?

---

<details style="background:#fff4f8;border:1px solid #efcad8;border-radius:16px;padding:16px;margin-top:18px;">
<summary><b>💡 Hint for Writing</b></summary>

Describe your family using:
✅ family members  
✅ adjectives  
✅ activities together  

---

### Helpful vocabulary

- <b>freundlich</b> = friendly  
- <b>lustig</b> = funny  
- <b>zusammen essen</b> = eat together  
- <b>zusammen reisen</b> = travel together  

---

### Strategy

Write:
1️⃣ who is in your family  
2️⃣ how they are  
3️⃣ what you do together  

</details>
