 ## 3.

---

<details style="background:#eef7ff;border:1px solid #cfe3ff;border-radius:16px;padding:16px;margin-top:18px;">
<summary><b>💡 Hint for Listening</b></summary>

In this listening task, you hear short information about Julian’s family.

🎯 Your job:
Listen carefully and decide:
- richtig = true
- falsch = false

---

### Listen carefully for:

✅ family members  
✅ names  
✅ descriptions  

---

### Helpful vocabulary

- <b>die Schwester</b> = sister  
- <b>die Mutter</b> = mother  
- <b>der Hund</b> = dog  
- <b>groß</b> = big/tall  
- <b>klein</b> = small  

---

### Strategy

1️⃣ Read the statements before listening.  

2️⃣ Underline important words:
- Schwester
- Mutter
- Hund

3️⃣ Listen for exact words and descriptions.

⚠️ Be careful:
Sometimes only ONE word changes the answer.

Example:
- groß ≠ klein

</details>

 ## 2.

---

<details style="background:#eefbea;border:1px solid #cfe7bf;border-radius:16px;padding:16px;margin-top:18px;">
<summary><b>💡 Hint for Reading</b></summary>

In this reading task, you read a short birthday text.

🎯 Focus on:
- Who comes?
- Where is the dog?

---

### Helpful vocabulary

- <b>Geburtstag</b> = birthday  
- <b>kommen</b> = to come  
- <b>der Garten</b> = garden  
- <b>das Haus</b> = house  

---

### Strategy

1️⃣ Read the whole text slowly.  

2️⃣ Find keywords from the questions.  

3️⃣ Compare the question with the text.

Example:
Text says:
👉 „Der Hund ist im Garten.“

Question:
👉 „Der Hund ist im Haus.“

❌ Different information = falsch

</details>

# ✍️ Schreiben – Teil 3

## 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Meine Familie

Schreiben Sie:

- Wie heißt Ihre Mutter?
- Haben Sie Geschwister?
- Wie ist Ihre Familie?

---

<details style="background:#fff4f8;border:1px solid #efcad8;border-radius:16px;padding:16px;margin-top:18px;">
<summary><b>💡 Hint for Writing</b></summary>

In this writing task, you write simple sentences about your family.

🎯 You should write:
1️⃣ your mother’s name  
2️⃣ brothers or sisters  
3️⃣ description of your family  

---

### Helpful vocabulary

- <b>Meine Mutter heißt ...</b> = My mother’s name is ...  
- <b>Ich habe ...</b> = I have ...  
- <b>einen Bruder</b> = a brother  
- <b>eine Schwester</b> = a sister  
- <b>meine Familie ist ...</b> = my family is ...  
- <b>nett</b> = nice  
- <b>groß</b> = big  

---

### Strategy

Write:
✅ one sentence about your mother  
✅ one sentence about siblings  
✅ one sentence about your family  

Use short A1 sentences.

</details>
