<details style="background:#eefbea;border:1px solid #cfe7bf;border-radius:14px;padding:14px;margin-bottom:18px;">
<summary><b>💡 Hint</b></summary>

In this reading task, you read a short university sports advertisement.

Focus on:
- sports activities
- price information
- registration details

---

### Helpful vocabulary

- <b>gratis</b> = free  
- <b>die Anmeldung</b> = registration  
- <b>online</b> = online  
- <b>das Sportprogramm</b> = sports program  

---

### Strategy

1️⃣ Read the text carefully.  

2️⃣ Look for important information:
- Is it free?
- Which sports are available?
- Do students need registration?

3️⃣ Compare the statements with the text.

---

### Important Clues

👉 “Eine Woche gratis”
= the first week is free

👉 “Anmeldung online”
= students must register online

---

🎯 Tip

Look carefully at words like:
- gratis
- online
- Anmeldung

These words help you answer correctly.
