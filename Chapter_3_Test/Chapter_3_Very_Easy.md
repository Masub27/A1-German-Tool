---

<details style="background:#fff4f8;border:1px solid #efcad8;border-radius:14px;padding:14px;margin-bottom:18px;">
<summary><b>💡 Hint</b></summary>

In this writing task, you want to go to the Mensa with your friend.

You should write:
1️⃣ When you want to go  
2️⃣ What you want to eat  
3️⃣ Where you want to meet  

---

### Helpful vocabulary

- <b>wollen wir ...?</b> = shall we ...?  
- <b>in die Mensa gehen</b> = go to the Mensa  
- <b>um 13 Uhr</b> = at 1 p.m.  
- <b>Pizza essen</b> = eat pizza  
- <b>vor der Mensa</b> = in front of the Mensa  
- <b>sich treffen</b> = to meet  

---

### Strategy

Write:
- one sentence with time
- one sentence about food
- one sentence about the meeting place

Use short and simple A1 sentences.

---

### Example Structure

👉 Wollen wir heute um 13 Uhr in die Mensa gehen?  
👉 Ich möchte Pizza essen.  
👉 Wir treffen uns vor der Mensa.
