---

<details style="background:#fff4f8;border:1px solid #efcad8;border-radius:14px;padding:14px;margin-bottom:18px;">
<summary><b>💡 Hint</b></summary>

In this writing task, you are at Café Campus and write a short message.

You should write:
1️⃣ What you want to drink  
2️⃣ What you want to eat  
3️⃣ Who is with you  

---

### Helpful vocabulary

- <b>Ich möchte ...</b> = I would like ...  
- <b>einen Tee</b> = a tea  
- <b>einen Espresso</b> = an espresso  
- <b>ein Franzbrötchen</b> = a cinnamon pastry  
- <b>mit Ben</b> = with Ben  
- <b>das Café</b> = the café  

---

### Strategy

Write:
- one sentence about the place
- one sentence about food and drinks
- one sentence about the person with you

Use short and simple A1 sentences.

---

### Example Structure

👉 Ich bin im Café Campus mit Ben.  
👉 Ich möchte einen Tee und ein Franzbrötchen.  
👉 Das Café ist sehr schön.

</details>
